Navigate online

Your desktop is the first thing that shows up at the startup. It's the core of your navigation on a computer. You can put documents, shortcuts for softwares and personnalize the appearrance.

At the bottom, the taskbar. It helps to navigate in the computer and achieves different tasks and look at wich apps are running.

On the left, there is the "start button that opens the star menu where all applications installed are displayed in the folders or in a list, on the left.
In the corner left, there is the power button where you can log out of your computer, meaning you're simply disconnecting your account.
you can also restart it or simply shut it down.

Next to the start button is a search bar. It helps to find an application or information on the internet, or on your computer.

The main part of the taskbar is the middle, where apps are pinned to the taskbar to access them easily and faster and also apps that are currently running.

On the right, you can find the date and the time section. When clicked, it shows the calendar (figure 1).
The sound section to control the volume of the device or, when right clicked, to open the volume mixer, where you can control all the volumes of the apps running (figure 2).
The network section shows the connection to internet and wi-fi networks (figure 3).
